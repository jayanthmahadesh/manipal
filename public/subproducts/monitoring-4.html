    RADIOMETER TCM COMBIM MONITOR
    The TCM CombiM Monitor provides real-time pO2 and pCO2 information continuously and non-invasively. Unique electrode technology provides accurate and rapid measurements which allow caregivers to better manage ventilator settings and ensure fast and immediate clinical intervention to critical changes. The TCM CombiM Monitor can be used on neonatal, pediatric and adult ICU patients.
